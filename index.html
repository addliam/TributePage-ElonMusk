BIOGRAPHY

Elon Musk born on June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa. Cofounded the electronic payment firm PayPal and formed SpaceX in 2002 and Tesla Motors in 2003.

At about the time of his parents’ divorce, when he was 10, Musk developed an interest in computers. He taught himself how to program, and when he was 12 he sold his first software: a game he created called Blastar.

In 1988, after obtaining a Canadian passport, Musk left South Africa because he was unwilling to support apartheid through compulsory military service and because he sought the greater economic opportunities available in the United States.
